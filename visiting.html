FAQs

What can i see and do there?
Crayola Experience is packed with 20+ creative activities and attractions where color, chemistry and technology magically combine to create a colorful adventure for your child. Enjoy a live show on how Crayola crayons are made. Explore Melt and Mold, where you can create everything with melted wax. Or create your very own Crayola crayon at Wrap It Up!
